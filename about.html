About Me

Hi, I'm the dev behind ZombieByte ☠

I'm an indie game developer obsessed with the golden era of 3D games — N64, PS1, PS2, GameCube. I make horror games that feel like forgotten cartridges found at a yard sale.

ZombieByte is my one-person studio. I handle everything: code, art, sound design, and way too much coffee.

The ZombieByte Story

ZombieByte started as a side project during a long weekend. One prototype turned into Guinda, a PS1-style horror game about a nun trapped in a monster-infested castle.

The name comes from two things I love: horror and technology. Zombie for the undead aesthetic, Byte for the retro digital soul.
